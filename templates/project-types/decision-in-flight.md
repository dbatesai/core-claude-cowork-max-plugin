# PROJECT.md — Decision in Flight Template

> **Template:** decision-in-flight.md — for an important decision being made, evaluated, or implemented.
>
> **Best for:** high-stakes decisions with meaningful consequences, multiple options, and stakeholders who need to agree or be informed. The DM's adversarial swarm mode adds the most value here — it will challenge your framing and surface options you might not have considered.
>
> **Recommended connectors:** Calendar (for deliberation meetings), Drive (for analysis documents and memos), Slack (for stakeholder threads).
>
