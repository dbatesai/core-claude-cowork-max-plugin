# PROJECT.md — Personal Project Template

> **Template:** personal.md — for a personal goal, side project, learning effort, or anything you're doing for yourself.
>
> **Best for:** goals with no team behind them, where the DM's job is to hold you accountable, surface what's getting in the way, and help you think clearly when it's just you against the blank page.
>
> **Recommended connectors:** Calendar (for time-blocking), Drive (for notes and drafts) — minimal. This template is designed to work well without any connectors at all.
